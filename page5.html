Why I crochet

posted on March 05,2025, at 6:38 PM Crocheting is one of the most amazing things I can do, and I think I can call myself an artist because of that. I get to make clothes, blankets, skirts, tousers, beanies etc. Crocheting is also one of the ways my family and I bond. Everytime we go on a family vacation, all the girls will bring their crochet stuff and we will all make something on the trip. I learned how to make pants on one trip to Wisconsin and I will never forget that.
